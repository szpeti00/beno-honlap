About us

dr. Benedek D. Varga Law Firm

The law firm is based on the recognition that, while every business needs sound legal advice, many believe that the traditional approach of law firms is outdated or at least not evolving fast enough. The ever-changing legal and economic environment of the modern world is indeed constantly evolving. Thus, changes require progressive thinking, creative, decisive, and often bold, but always reliable and stable legal solutions. This is the approach and practice that our law firm applies in its work. We love working with people; our clients' business success inspires us, and we are proud to be part of their success.

dr. Benedek D. Varga, attorney at law

dr. Benedek D. Varga, attorney at law

Benedek graduated from the Faculty of Law and Political Sciences of Pázmány Péter Catholic University in 2017. Following this, he worked for four years as a trainee lawyer at Pozderka & Partners Law Firm, where he mainly worked on commercial, tort, and insurance dispute resolution. Later, he became an in-house law counsel for one of Hungary's long-established construction companies. The law firm was founded to achieve success for its clients by providing comprehensive legal advice and litigation representation in a wide range of civil and commercial law areas, with the involvement of partner financial advisors, thus providing a service that is in tune with the ever-changing economic and legal environment. The law firm is a member of the Csurgó and Pozderka Partnership Law Offices. The team of collaborating lawyers has over 100 years of combined experience in insurance law, all having worked for many years as lawyers, managers and senior executives in multinational insurance companies.

Zoltán Varga, leader of the financial staff accounting

Zoltán Varga, leader of the financial staff accounting

Zoltán has worked in finance, tax, accounting, bookkeeping, and business consulting for more than 22 years. Zoltán supports our legal and individual clients as a financial advisor and accountant in the law firm, helping to deliver well-prepared legal and financial solutions.

Professions

Economic and Company Law

Our firm provides complete legal and financial services in the formation, operation, transformation, dissolution, and acquisition of business organisations. These services include compliance procedures, legal representation in M&A transactions, and the legal and financial aspects of due diligence procedures.

Dispute Resolution

It is a universal truth that all disputes should be settled by compromise. We strive to resolve our clients' disputes in a way that minimises risk and has the least possible impact on our clients' lives and businesses.

In civil litigation, we defend our clients' interests by applying litigation tactics based on careful analysis and involving experts with expertise in the subject matter of the litigation.

Claims Management

Our office enforces our clients' outstanding by initiating the most effective out-of-court and litigation procedures.

Insurance Law

Our office provides a wide range of services in insurance law, including developing and auditing policies and contractual systems that govern the day-to-day operations of our clients' internal processes. We also fully represent our clients before Hungarian dispute resolution forums.

Tort Law

Our firm also represents injured parties and claimants in litigation and out-of-court proceedings in personal injury, occupational accidents, building, motor vehicle and other property damage cases.

Property Law - Legal Representation of Condominiums

Our activities include the sale and purchase of residential and industrial properties, the drafting of lease contracts, articles of association, organisational and operational rules, general assembly resolutions, and legal representation in related litigation.

Construction Law

We support our clients in construction projects, from drafting the contractual arrangements for the project to handling claims during the warranty and guarantee period.

Data Protection and Data Security Law

Keeping up to date with data protection and security is essential for a successful business. Our audit process, provided together with our cybersecurity partner, assesses the data protection and data security readiness and risks of companies. It will then make necessary amendments and updates to the policies and IT systems.

Our firm provides ongoing strategic and ad hoc advice to help our clients find the balance between respecting the privacy rights of data subjects and pursuing their own economic interests, which is essential for development.

Law of Inheritance

Our office helps clients navigate the system of succession law.

Our services include drafting succession agreements, advising on wills and representing heirs and estate creditors in probate proceedings.

Working with our fiduciary partners, we can support our clients in preserving and growing their family wealth and businesses for generations.

Economic Criminal Law

Economic crimes and their consequences represent a serious risk for businesses. Proper identification, evaluation and mitigation of these risks is always an expectation of the shareholders towards the management.

In representing plaintiffs in economic criminal cases, our firm correctly identifies and assesses the risk caused by the crime and prepares a complex proposal for the management. Thus, we prepare and facilitate the appropriate decisions and implementation to avoid the consequences of the crime.

Crimes Against Property

We also represent defendants and plaintiffs in infringement and criminal proceedings regarding crimes against property.

Traffic Offences

Our office also acts for and represents defendants and plaintiffs in infringement and criminal proceedings regarding traffic offences.
